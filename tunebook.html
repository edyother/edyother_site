Tunebook

I started a project a few years ago of transcribing my repertoire of tunes. Here's where that's at today. It's organized so that each page I a set that I either currently play, have played at some point in the past, or learned together as a set. For this reason you'll find some of the tunes are reapeated. As for the order that the sets are in... Essentially they're in the order that I learned them. In that way this also serves a somewhat of a jounal to me. If you look the abc file that this tunebook is made from you'll occasionally see additional information in some of the source headers (S:) on where I learned a tune or set.
