Ed's Tunebook

I started a project around 2012 of transcribing my repertoire of tunes. Here's where that's at today. It's organized so that each page I a set that I either currently play, have played at some point in the past, or learned together as a set. As for the order that the sets are in... Essentially they're in the order that I learned them. In that way this also serves as somewhat of a journal to me.
